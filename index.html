Age-Appropriate Privacy Practices

For Children Under 13

We are fully committed to complying with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the Google Play Families Policy.

Key Privacy Protections for Children:
  • Non-Personalized Ads Only: Children only see context-based advertisements that do not track or profile them.
  • No Advertising IDs: We do not collect or transmit advertising IDs (AAID) for children.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect the minimum data necessary to operate the app.
  • Certified Ad Networks: We only use advertising SDKs certified by the Google Play Families Self-Certified Ads SDK Program for child users.
  • Parental Consent: We obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information from children.

For Users 13 and Older

Adult users (13+) experience standard advertising practices with personalized ads to provide a more relevant experience:

  • Personalized Ads: Ads may be tailored based on interests and app usage patterns.
  • Advertising ID Collection: We collect the Advertising ID (AAID) for ad personalization.
  • User Consent: We obtain consent for personalized advertising and data collection as required by applicable laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.).
  • Opt-Out Options: Adult users can opt out of personalized advertising through device settings or app preferences.

How We Determine User Age

To provide age-appropriate privacy protections:

  • Age Verification: We ask users to provide their age or date of birth during first use.
  • Parental Gate: For users who indicate they are under 13, we implement a parental gate mechanism to ensure a parent or guardian is involved.
  • Default Protection: Until age is verified, we treat all users as children under 13 and apply the strictest privacy protections.
  • No Age Changes: Once set, user age category cannot be changed without parental verification (for children) or account reset.

Data Collection and Use

For Children Under 13

What We Do NOT Collect from Children

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Photos, audio, or video recordings
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Advertising IDs (AAID) for ad personalization
  • Browsing history or behavioral profiles
  • Any other personally identifiable information

What We DO Collect from Children (with parental consent)

  • Age Information: Date of birth or age range for age-appropriate content and privacy protection.
  • App Performance Data: Basic information about app functionality, such as crashes and errors.
  • Anonymous Usage Statistics: Non-identifiable information about which features are used.
  • Device Information: Basic device type and operating system version for compatibility purposes only.

For Users 13 and Older

What We Collect from Adults

  • Age Information: Date of birth or age range for age verification and content personalization.
  • Advertising ID (AAID): For personalized advertising and analytics.
  • App Performance Data: Information about app functionality, crashes, and errors.
  • Usage Data: Information about app interactions, features used, quiz performance, and preferences.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, screen resolution, language settings.
  • Approximate Location: Country and region based on IP address for regional content and ads (not precise geolocation).
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information about ads viewed and clicked for ad optimization.

How We Use Adult User Data

  • To provide personalized advertising experiences
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To analyze app performance and user engagement
  • To provide customer support
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security

Advertising

For Children Under 13: Non-Personalized Ads Only

Children see only non-personalized, context-based advertisements:

  • Context-Based: Ads are based on app content, not user behavior or profile.
  • Age-Appropriate: All ads comply with COPPA and Google Play's child-directed content policies.
  • No Tracking: We do not allow ad networks to track, profile, or build advertising profiles of children.
  • No Persistent Identifiers: Advertising IDs are not collected or transmitted for children.

For Users 13 and Older: Personalized Ads

Adult users receive personalized advertisements based on:

  • Interest-Based Targeting: Ads tailored to user interests and preferences.
  • Behavioral Data: App usage patterns and interaction history.
  • Demographic Information: Age group and region.
  • Advertising ID: Device advertising identifier for cross-app ad personalization.

Opt-Out Options for Adults

Adult users can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Device Settings: Adjust ad preferences in device settings (Android: Settings > Google > Ads; iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising)
  • Reset Advertising ID: Reset your advertising identifier to break the link with previous ad interactions
  • App Settings: Toggle ad personalization preferences within the app settings menu
  • Premium Upgrade: Purchase the ad-free version to remove all advertisements

Our Advertising Provider

We use Google AdMob for serving advertisements. AdMob provides:

  • Non-personalized ads for children under 13 (COPPA compliant)
  • Personalized ads for users 13 and older (with consent)
  • Certification under the Google Play Families Self-Certified Ads SDK Program

Data Shared with AdMob for Children (non-personalized ads):

  • General device information (device type, OS version) - NOT for ad targeting
  • IP address (used only for fraud prevention and approximate geographic location, not for tracking)
  • App interaction data (e.g., when an ad is displayed) - NOT linked to any user identity

Data Shared with AdMob for Adults (personalized ads):

  • Advertising ID (AAID)
  • Device information (type, OS version, screen size)
  • IP address for approximate location
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Ad interaction history (views, clicks)
  • Inferred interests and demographics

For more information about AdMob's privacy practices, visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements (non-personalized for children, personalized for adults)
  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases (premium upgrade to remove ads)
